Some of the famous forts in Maharashtra include Raigad Fort, Shivneri Fort, Harehareshawer Fort, Pratapgad Fort,Rajgad Fort,Panhala Fort and many more.

1.Raigad Fort

    Raigad is one of the historically significant forts in Maharashtra. Nestled amid the picturesque Sahyadri mountain ranges in Maharashtra. The Raigarh Fort stands tall as a symbol of historical grandeur and architectural brilliance. This ancient fort, with its rich heritage and breathtaking views, beckons history enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.
      In this article, we will take you on a journey to discover the captivating essence of the Raigarh Fort, covering its history, location, timings, and the fascinating stories that lie within its walls.

Location: Raigad, Maharashtra

Timings: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Nearest Railway Station: Dasgaon Railway Station

Nearest Airport: Lohegaon Airport, Pune

Famous For: Sightseeing, Trekking, Photography


2.Rajgad Fort

Rajgad Fort, located in Maharashtra, India, is a majestic hill fortress with a history tracing back to the 17th century. Originally known as Murumdev, it was later captured and expanded by the Maratha king Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, who made it his capital.
The fort's strategic position atop the Sahyadri mountains and its impregnable defenses made it a symbol of Maratha power and resistance against invaders. Rajgad Fort stands as a significant historical landmark, representing the valor and strategic acumen of the Maratha Empire during its reign.

Location: Pune, Maharashtra

Timings: Open 24 Hours

Nearest Railway Station: Veer Dasgaon Railway Station

Nearest Airport: Lohegaon Airport, Pune

Famous For: Trekking, Photography & Sightseeing


3.Panhala Fort

Panhala Fort in Maharashtra is an ancient hill fort with a rich history dating back to the 12th century. It was initially built by the Shilahara dynasty and later fortified by various rulers, including the Marathas and the   Bahamani Sultanate. This massive citadel served as a strategic military outpost and witnessed numerous battles due to its commanding position in the Sahyadri mountain range.

Panhala Fort's impressive architecture and historical significance make it a prominent heritage site, attracting visitors who seek to explore its intriguing past and enjoy the breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Location: Kolhapur District, Maharashtra

Timings: 6:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Nearest Railway Station: Kolhapur Railway Station

Nearest Airport: Kolhapur Airport

Famous For: Trekking, Photography & Sightseeing


4.Shivneri Fort

Shivneri Fort, located in Maharashtra, is a historic gem and the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Perched atop a hill near Junnar, it boasts stunning medieval architecture with seven gates and a revered temple dedicated to Goddess Shivai Devi.
   The fort offers captivating trails and breathtaking views, attracting history enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.       The Shivneri Fort welcomes visitors to witness the majestic Ambarkhana and immerse themselves in the legacy of one of India's greatest warriors.

Location: Pune, Maharashtra

Timings: 6:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Nearest Railway Station: Pune Railway Station

Nearest Airport: Lohegaon Airport, Pune

Famous For: Trekking, Photography & Sightseeing


5.Pratapgad Fort

Pratapgad Fort, located in Maharashtra, is a historically significant fortress known for its pivotal role in the Battle of Pratapgad between the Maratha Empire and the Adil Shahi Sultanate. Built in the 17th century, the fort's strategic location atop a hill provided a vantage point for the Marathas during the battle.

Its sturdy architecture, including the Bhavani and Mahadev temples, showcases the Maratha's engineering prowess. Pratapgad Fort in Maharashtra stands as a symbol of Maratha valor and triumph, attracting history enthusiasts keen to explore this fascinating chapter in India's history.

Location: Pune, Maharashtra

Timings: 6:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Nearest Railway Station: Satara Railway Station

Nearest Airport: Lohegaon Airport, Pune

Famous For: Trekking, Photography & Sightseeing


6.Harehareshawer Fort

Harihar Fort, located in Nashik, Maharashtra, India, is a historic hill fort dating back to the Yadava dynasty. It stands at an elevation of 1,120 meters above sea level and is famous for its unique rock-cut steps leading to the summit, offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

The fort has a rich history and was strategically important during various periods of rule in the region. Today, it is a popular trekking destination for adventure enthusiasts and history buffs alike. Location: Nashik, Maharashtra

Timings: 6:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Nearest Railway Station: Nashik Railway Station

Nearest Airport: Ozar Airport(Nashik Airport), Nashik

Famous For: Trekking, Photography & Sightseeing
