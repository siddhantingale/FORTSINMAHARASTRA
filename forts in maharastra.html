Forts Of Maharashtra: Overview

Some of the famous forts in Maharashtra include Raigad Fort, Shivneri Fort,Torna Fort, Harehareshawer Fort, Pratapgad Fort, Rajgad Fort and many more. Known for its rich history and the land of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the forts in Maharashtra represent the glorious past of the Maratha Empire. There are around 350 forts in Maharashtra of different dynasties, trading centres, Marathas, and Mughals. Many of the forts in Maharashtra are located in the Sahyadri mountains i.e. Western Ghats in Maharashtra. The Forts in Maharashtra offer breathtaking views surrounding the landscape and are a popular tourist attraction that attracts history enthusiasts, trekkers and nature lovers.
